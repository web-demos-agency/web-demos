The Ultimate FAQ

01. What does it cost in 2026?

In Brisbane and the Gold Coast, a custom concrete pool build typically ranges from $60,000 to $120,000+. The "Elite" factor comes from site accessibility, hydraulic engineering complexity, and your choice of finishes. We provide a transparent line-item quote with zero hidden surprises.

  • Council Permits: $3k - $5k
  • Engineering & Earthworks: $8k - $15k
  • Filtration & Smart Gear: $7k - $12k

02. How long to swim?

Quality cannot be rushed. Our typical build timeline is 12 to 16 weeks from permit approval. Concrete requires a "curing phase" of 28 days to reach maximum strength before we apply high-end quartz or glass internals.

Why Concrete is the King of Gold Coast Real Estate

In the premium suburbs of the Gold Coast and Brisbane, a concrete pool is more than a swimming hole—it's a high-yield asset. Unlike fiberglass shells which have limited shapes and depth, concrete allows for infinity edges, sun-shelves, and custom benches that fit your landscape perfectly. From an investment perspective, concrete pools add significantly more equity to your property. Architectural integration is key: a pool should look like an extension of the house, not an afterthought. We've seen properties with Eco Pools concrete builds sell for 15-20% above suburb medians.

Magnesium Minerals: The Future of Chlorine-Free Life

Traditional chlorine can cause skin irritation, red eyes, and a toxic smell. At Eco Pools, we believe in a better way. Magnesium mineral systems don't just sanitize your water; they offer a therapeutic experience. Magnesium is naturally detoxifying and relaxing for muscles. When you swim in one of our magnesium concrete pools, you feel recharged, not chemical-soaked. It's safe for children with sensitive skin and eco-friendly for the surrounding environment. Our specialized pumps and filters are designed to work with these minerals to maintain crystal clarity with minimal human intervention.
