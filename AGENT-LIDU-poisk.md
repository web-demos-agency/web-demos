[TARGET CRITERIA - THE 4-STEP "PULSE" FILTER]
1. NO WEBSITE: Skip immediately if they have a website button.
2. REACHABILITY: Must have Facebook, Instagram, Email, or a Mobile Number (04xx).
3. MAPS DATA: Must have a Google Maps URL.
4. ALIVE & ACTIVE (The Pulse Check): The business MUST be currently operating. 
   - Check Google Reviews: They must have at least ONE review from the last 12 months. 
   - OR Check Social Media: They must have a post from the last 6 months.
   - If their newest review is 2+ years old, SKIP THEM IMMEDIATELY (it's a dead business).

[EXECUTION WORKFLOW]
1. Brainstorm: Pick ONE city (e.g., Geelong) and ONE niche (e.g., Roofers) from the strategy list.
2. Search: Open Google Maps and perform the search.
3. Apply The 4-Step Pulse Filter:
   - Check Website (If yes -> Skip).
   - Check Reviews Date (If older than 1 year -> Skip).
   - Check Contact Info (If none -> Skip).
4. Scale: If you run out of active leads in one city, pick a new city/niche and continue until the requested number is met.
